Effects of Marijuana

The legalization of marijuana has been studied in various aspects, Marijuana can be beneficial to health and stimulate the brain with euphoric feeling. Nonetheless, it might lead to other illicit drug use. More analyses regarding health risks of marijuana are required. To help stakeholders better manage and understand marijuana legalization, it is necessary to illustrate the subject in a more direct and accessible manner. Also, detailed evaluations on the impact of marijuana, such as substance use and mental health status, are essential.
